Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2026 Applies worldwide

Short version: BopBooth processes all your photos locally in your browser. We never upload, store, or transmit your camera feed or photos to any server. The only data we collect is standard analytics and advertising cookies from Google.

1. Information We Collect

Camera & Photos

BopBooth requests access to your device's camera only when you click "Take Photos." Your camera feed is processed entirely on your device using the browser's Canvas API. No images are ever transmitted to our servers. When you close or refresh the page, all captured images are permanently discarded.

Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites, we collect standard non-personal information including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL and pages visited on our site
  • Date and time of your visit
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)

This data is collected via Google Analytics and is aggregated it cannot be used to identify you personally.

No Account or Registration

BopBooth does not offer user accounts. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal information unless you voluntarily contact us (see Section 7).

2. How We Use Information

The limited data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use the site (page views, popular features)
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Monitor and improve site performance and reliability
  • Detect and prevent abuse or technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate site usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data about how visitors use BopBooth. This data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google Fonts

We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may log your IP address when the fonts are served. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Unsplash

Sample images displayed on the landing page are served from Unsplash's CDN. Your browser will make requests to Unsplash's servers when loading these images. See Unsplash's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. BopBooth uses the following cookies:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the site to function and cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

These cookies collect anonymous information about how you use the site pages visited, session duration, and general traffic patterns. The data is aggregated and does not identify individuals.

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

Google AdSense sets cookies to show relevant ads. These cookies may track your browsing activity across sites to serve personalised advertisements. You can manage your ad preferences at Google Ad Settings.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality and the relevance of displayed ads. For more information visit aboutcookies.org.

5. Your Rights

GDPR European Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right of access request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing request limitation of how we process your data
  • Right to data portability receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object object to data processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing

Since we do not store personal data, most of these rights are satisfied by design. To exercise any right, contact us at support@bopbooth.com.

CCPA California Users

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you rights including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, email support@bopbooth.com.

6. Data Security

Because BopBooth processes all photos locally and does not transmit them to any server, the primary security mechanism is your browser's built-in sandboxing. We serve the website over HTTPS to protect data in transit. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

BopBooth is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us, please contact us at support@bopbooth.com and we will take steps to delete such information. BopBooth complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of BopBooth after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@bopbooth.com
  • Response time: within 48 hours
