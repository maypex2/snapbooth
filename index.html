How it works

No app to install. No account to create. Just open your browser and start shooting.

1

Allow Camera Access

Open the photo booth and click "Take Photos". Your browser will ask for camera permission that's all the setup you need.

2

Pick Your Style

Choose a filter, select a frame theme, and get ready. A 3-second countdown fires automatically between each shot.

3

Download & Share

Your photo strip is generated instantly. Download as a PNG or animated GIF and share it anywhere no watermark.
